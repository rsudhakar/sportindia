Event Details
Singaram Pillai AITA tennis tournament 2015 is a championship series for the children under age group 14 for both boys and girls
Rules and Regulations
  • Players must show their proof of age while sign-in
  • Players must be prepared to play more than 2 matches a day
  • Current AITA rules will be strictly followed
  • The chief referee's decision is final
  • Telephone and SMS entry will not be accepted
  • It is mandatory for the player to carry ORIGINAL REGISTRATION CARD for the sign-in. In case the player registration is in process with AITA, In that case player has to carry Original receipt or copy of mail from AITA confirming that player registration is in process
